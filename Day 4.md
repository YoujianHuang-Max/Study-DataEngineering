---

## 2. ETL Concept
**ETL** is a common data engineering workflow:

1. **Extract** – Read data from source files (CSV, JSON, database, etc.)  
2. **Transform** – Clean and process data to ensure quality and consistency  
3. **Load** – Save processed data to a destination (CSV, Parquet, database, etc.)
